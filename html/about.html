About

Since I first got a desktop when I was little I became fascinated with the universe that exists inside computers. For a while I was too afraid to really follow my dreams and I tried pursuing a career in marketing. My true calling was always software and when I applied to Aalborg University at a master’s about IT Design and Application Development I finally took a leap of faith and started on my dream career path. Now that I am finally on this path I aim to create software that helps people while also taking on challenges that help me grow and test my limits.

During my master’s I realized the perfect working environment for me involves interactions with other diverse minds. I am equally passionate about programming as I am about design and I believe good products come from simple and clear solutions.

My background in both the social sciences and software development allows me to understand users from a unique point of view. My work is guided by responsability and ethics, with emphasis on always enjoying the process and striving to achieve the best outcomes.

When I am not working I love to try new things and constantly learn and improve. My favorite leisure activities include either watching a good movie. reading a captivating book or simply enjoying some video games.
